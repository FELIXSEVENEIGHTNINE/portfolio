Accountrix

Accountrix is an accounting game that trains and teaches Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) students the core basics of accounting.

The project was first in under 1 week, but asked for extensions due to project timing hitting my personal schedule. I managed to finish one of the hardest functionality in the game in under a month.
