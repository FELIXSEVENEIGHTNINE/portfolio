A Minute to Spare

A Minute to Spare is inspired by a game called 20 Minute Till Dawn.

Majority of the game design was based on that game. You can upgrade your character and weapon every one (1) minute.

Upgrades and upgrade UI was based from Team Fortress 2.

Currently, there are 3 different enemies: an average enemy with average health, a large enemy with a lot of health that spawns every even number of rounds, and a very fast but very weak enemy that spawns every odd number of rounds.

Currently, there are two playable weapons: a shotgun and a gatling gun.

Some of the music uses Scratch's default library, while some are from Fallout 4's battle themes slowed and pitched down.

Tools used:

  • Scratch 3.0
  • TurboWarp
