A Minute to Spare

A Minute to Spare is inspired by a game called 20 Minute Till Dawn.

Majority of the game design was based on that game. You can upgrade your character and weapon every one (1) minute.

Upgrade game design and upgrade UI was based from Team Fortress 2

Tools used:

  • Scratch 3.0
  • TurboWarp

Features:

  • Local IP addresses are automatically stored. This is a positive for users who does not want to disconnect their sessions from the server.
  • Users can store passwords, bookmarks, and files.
  • Users can register and log in to retrieve their stuff.
