A Minute to Spare

A Minute to Spare is a rogue-link top-down shooter that spawns in waves of enemies every minute.

This game was inspired by 20 Minutes Til Dawn. I really like games that allow you to upgrade while you're under pressure.

The upgrade system was based on Team Fortress 2's Mann vs Machine gamemode.

The development took almost 2 years, and another year balancing and remaking parts.

Game Link: https://gamejolt.com/games/amin2spare/748475

Tools used:

  • Scratch 3.0 for the initial development
  • TurpoWarp, a tool using Scratch, to continue the increasing needed functionalities

Features:

  • Players start with some amount to money to upgrade them or their weapon.
  • Bats (unintentional enemy during the pandemic) were the common type of enemies. Over the course of the game they spawn faster.
  • Butterflies are enemies with higher health, taking two shotgun shots to take down, but they are slower than the Bats.
  • Dragonflies are the fastest enemies in the game taking only 1.5 seconds to reach the player.
